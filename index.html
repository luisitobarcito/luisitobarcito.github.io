Basic Info:

My research interest are the intersection of machine learning, computational neuroscience and their application to computer vision and signal processing. My current research focuses on develop information theoretic measures and approaches applied to the training of machine learning models as well as understanding how the brain process information. I am particularly interested in how information can be represented to integrate multiple data modalities and how this gives rise to intelligent behavior.
